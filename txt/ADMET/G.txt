Here's the brief report on G.

1. Absorption:
   - G is orally administered and readily absorbed. 
   - In the presence of gastric acid, G rapidly dissociates.

2. Metabolism:
   - G is primarily metabolized in the liver through hydroxylation.
   - The metabolism process mainly relies on the cytochrome enzyme.

3. Toxicity:
   - G is generally considered a relatively safe drug.
   - Short term use usually does not produce serious toxic side effects.
